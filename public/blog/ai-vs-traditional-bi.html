What is Traditional Business Intelligence?

Traditional BI platforms collect enterprise data and display it using dashboards, reports, and charts. These systems rely heavily on historical data and manual interpretation.

Common traditional BI capabilities include:

  • Dashboard visualization
  • Financial reporting
  • Operational reporting
  • Data aggregation
  • Manual analysis

However, traditional BI systems cannot predict future outcomes or automatically identify root causes.

What is AI-Driven Analytics?

AI-driven analytics platforms use machine learning, predictive analytics, and AI decision intelligence to analyze enterprise data automatically.

These platforms can:

  • Predict business outcomes
  • Detect anomalies automatically
  • Perform root cause analysis
  • Recommend strategic actions
  • Automate decision processes
